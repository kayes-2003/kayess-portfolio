Memories at JUSC PATHFINDER-Unlick your potential

02/28/2025 · 1 min read

I am delighted to share my experience serving as the Convenor of the impactful program “JUSC PATHFINDER: Unlock Your Potential.” The event was designed to empower participants with essential academic and professional skills while encouraging confidence, creativity, and collaboration.

The program featured several engaging segments, including an exciting Treasure Hunt that promoted teamwork and problem-solving, a 4-Minute Presentation session to enhance public speaking abilities, and practical workshops on Academic CV preparation and professional email writing—skills that are crucial for career growth.

Being the convenor allowed me to strengthen my leadership, organizational, and communication skills while working closely with an amazing team to make the event successful. Seeing the enthusiasm and active participation of attendees made the entire journey truly rewarding. Grateful for the experience and proud to contribute to a platform that helps individuals discover their potential and prepare for future opportunities.
