Hello, it's me
      KAYES

Growing up in Rajshahi, I was always fascinated by how small ideas could turn into something bigger when shared with the right people. Moving to Dhaka for university opened up a whole new world of possibilities — the city’s energy pushed me to explore coding, writing, and creative projects with fresh ambition.

During my early days at Jahangirnagar University’s Statistics and Data Science department, I spent countless evenings in the campus library tinkering with Bootstrap layouts and drafting blog posts that blended technical insights with storytelling. One late night, after losing track of time debugging a project, I realized that this mix of problem‑solving and creative expression was exactly where I belonged.

Today, as an young boy still carving my path, I see every project and every piece of writing as a step toward building digital spaces that feel authentic, engaging, and true to my journey.
